  • Circut - lightbulb

    This circut was made with two jumper wires, one resistor, one lightbulb in addition to the breadboard and battery. This was made with a lot of trial and error. Along the way, I discovered that the 5 pins in the same horizontal row were connected. The circut was not working at first because the circut was not "closed." Once the pins were aligned in the rows, the circut was closed and the light turned on. Though we burned out two bulbs along the way, we successfully completed the task.
